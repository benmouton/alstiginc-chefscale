C1 — Stainless & Zinc

Pure zinc/steel palette (#18181b backgrounds, #27272a borders). Orange (#f97316) as the operational accent — like warning labels on kitchen equipment. Sharp 6-8px corners. Category colors only on labels, not full card gradients. Data-dense: cost/serving visible on every card. Monospace for numbers. Feels like a POS terminal for food.

C2 — Line Ticket

Inspired by kitchen order tickets. Left accent stripe per category (like a colored ticket rail). Structured data layout — servings, time, cost, food% in labeled columns. Slightly warmer black (#1c1c1e). Orange accent stays but sharper. Feels like a kitchen pass with ticket rails.

C3 — Kitchen Ledger

Inspired by spreadsheets and kitchen logs. Table-row layout with column headers (Recipe, Srv, Cost, FC%). Maximum data density — see every recipe's economics at a glance. Underlined tab filters instead of pills. Feels like a digital kitchen clipboard. Most information per pixel of any option.
